About

Hi there! I am Plácido. Welcome to my personal site.

I am a Computing Engineer doing science at CERN. I work on design, architecture and development of software for future detectors for High Energy Physics.

I did my PhD in Computer Science and Technology at CERN, in the LHCb experiment, with University Carlos III of Madrid.
